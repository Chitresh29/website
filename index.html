About Us

Want to condense your sales cycles, reduce cost per customer sign up, and improve customer satisfaction & retention? mquotient is building the solutions you need to succeed.

In today’s mobile world, instant is the operating word, we are taking laborious manual processes in your customer engagement process out of the equation.

Our products are highly scalable and can be deployed locally inside your network or hosted on some of the most secure cloud networks. Open APIs allow seamless connection to your existing systems for pushing and pulling data.

We have offices in Pune and New Delhi, reach us on info@mquotient.net for more information.

Products

Glyfix

Glyfix is an artificial intelligence based application using word recognition and capture technologies to convert hand written text into digital format. By automating manual data entry, Glyfix dramatically reduces the response time to customers, improves accuracy and directly boosts the bottom line.

LMS

LMS is an integrated marketing and sales platform to record, organize, action, analyze and report customer data to enable enterprises to drive profits from multi-channel marketing programs.

Want to join Us?

What do we do?


You know the times which left your wondering why your Number Portability application took 2 weeks before you shifted providers or when your bank account became operational 13 days after you applied for it, the programs we write resolve issues as these.


Our broad vision is to provide customer experience solutions which drastically reduce turnaround time for companies by automating mundane manual processes. Businesses should not only get back to you immediately, they should also give you an experience that’s more efficient, consistent and personalized across multiple touch points as Web, Field Agents, Retail & Mobile.


Look into the solutions we are working on, if the problems we are solving are challenging for you, check out the openings we have currently.



Do you fit in?


We are looking for people who solve issues around them.


  • Do you generally punch above your weight?
  • Are you willing to give all that it takes to support your team?
  • Do you get frustrated by getting scrutinized through multiple layers of bureaucracy?


If so, we’d like to hear from you. We’re building outstanding customer experience products and need you to help us build it the right way, drop in a note to Vijisha @ vijisha@mquotient.net, with samples of some recent work you are proud of.



What you’d be doing?


  1. You will be working on our labs projects where we do all our experiments and inventions. These are rapid prototyping where we do amazing innovations. It is an absolute software playground and we let you build grand castles.
  2. You will be writing production ready code which will be deployed in the servers of some of the largest companies in the world. These will be highly scalable and robust code with an expected uptime of more than 99.99%. Don't worry, we will also teach you how to do that.
  3. You will be required to keep yourself up to date on everything new in the software. You will be given topics or languages to learn and teach your colleagues.
