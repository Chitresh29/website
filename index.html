About Us

Want to condense your sales cycles, reduce cost per customer sign up, and improve customer satisfaction & retention? mquotient is building the solutions you need to succeed.

In today’s mobile world, instant is the operating word, we are taking laborious manual processes in your customer engagement process out of the equation.

Our products are highly scalable and can be deployed locally inside your network or hosted on some of the most secure cloud networks. Open APIs allow seamless connection to your existing systems for pushing and pulling data.


Products

GlyphX


GlyphX is a customer onboarding solution which automates the manual data and business processes in creating and managing customers. It uses ICR (Intelligent Character Recognition) and Artificial Intelligence algorithms to convert handwritten and printed documents to digital format

The solution allows data processing in near- Real time. The ability to scale instantly allows processing the peak volumes without any delay.


Boxcite


Boxcite is an integrated marketing and sales platform to record, organize, action, analyze and report customer data to enable enterprises to drive profits from multi-channel marketing programs. Boxcite is built using open source technologies and integrates easily with various CRM, ERP and legacy systems. The applications of Boxcite are several – as a data mart integrating data from multiple sources, for multi-channel demand generation, sales automation, CRM, performance analysis and campaign management.
